Client Management

Complete client profiles with health history, dietary restrictions, consent tracking, and contact information — all in one accessible view.

  • Detailed health & dietary profiles
  • Consent capture & tracking
  • Searchable client directory
  • Intake form management

Appointment Scheduling

Streamlined booking and session management. See your day at a glance and never miss a follow-up with smart scheduling tools.

  • Calendar view with daily schedule
  • Session type management

Why Nutrir

Built differently, on purpose

Not another generic CRM repurposed for health. Nutrir is designed from the ground up for how nutritionists actually work.

Command Center Dashboard

See your entire practice at a glance — today's schedule, client alerts, active meal plans, and recent activity in one dense, actionable view.

Privacy by Default

Built for Canadian health privacy law from day one. PIPEDA, PHIPA, and HIA requirements are baked into the architecture, not bolted on after.

Solo Practitioner Focus

No enterprise bloat. No features you'll never use. Nutrir is laser-focused on the workflows of independent dietitians and nutritionists.

Compliance & Security

Your clients' trust is non-negotiable

Handling personal health information requires more than good intentions. Nutrir implements the safeguards your profession demands.

Canadian Privacy Compliance
Purpose-built for PIPEDA, PHIPA, and HIA. Consent capture, data minimization, and breach notification workflows included.
Full Audit Trail
Every data access and modification is logged. Know who accessed what, when, and why — essential for regulatory reviews.
Encryption & Access Control
HTTPS/HSTS enforced, multi-factor authentication, and role-based access control. Your data is protected at rest and in transit.
